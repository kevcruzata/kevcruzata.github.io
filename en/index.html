Hey there! I’m Kevin Cruzata — or just Kev. I started my journey as a teacher in the Philippines and quickly realized I love turning learning into an adventure. In 2018, I moved to Italy, dove into new cultures and languages, and eventually found myself in Switzerland, where I began pursuing a long-time curiosity: code and design. These days, I'm actively studying and training in tech — learning, building, breaking things (on purpose), and loving every step.

Off screen, you’ll find me singing, wandering in nature, or hanging out with my cats Charlie and Soleil. I geek out over languages — human and computer — and I live by three things: discover, learn, enjoy.
