Hey there! I’m Kevin Cruzata — or Kev. I was born in the Philippines in 1996, and my journey has been anything but linear. I began my professional life as a teacher, where I discovered a love for explaining concepts, building connections, and making learning meaningful. After graduating, I moved to Italy and immersed myself in new languages, cultures, and challenges that expanded both my mindset and my skillset.

I moved recently to Ticino, Switzerland, and felt it was the right moment to take a new step — to finally study and pursue the path I had been curious about for years. That curiosity and love for learning led me to computer science. I’m currently enrolled in CS50x through HarvardX, taking my first solid steps into programming, web development, and all the problem-solving that comes with it. At fall, I’ll begin my formal journey in Computer Engineering. I’m still figuring out my place in this vast field, but I know I’m where I’m supposed to be — building something new.

Outside of the screen, I’m drawn to things that spark joy and connection: singing, animals, nature, traveling, and learning languages — both human and computer. Whether in the classroom or vs code, I’ve always believed in one thing: discover, learn, enjoy.
