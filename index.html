Hey there! I’m Kevin Cruzata — or just Kev.

Born in the Philippines in 1996, my path has zigzagged a bit (in the best way). I started out teaching — and discovered I love turning learning into an adventure. In 2018, I packed my bags for Italy, soaked up the languages and culture, and stretched my brain in new directions. Fast forward to now — I’m in Ticino, Switzerland, and finally chasing something I’ve been curious about for years: code and design.

I’m currently enrolled in CS50x through HarvardX and gearing up to start Computer Engineering this fall. I'm still figuring out where I fit in this massive world of tech — but I’m building, breaking, learning, and loving it.

When I’m off screen, you’ll find me singing, hanging out with Charlie and Soleil (yes, cats), exploring nature, hopping countries, or geeking out over languages (both human and computer). Big believer in three things: discover, learn, enjoy.
